  • Fall of Giants
    • Amidst the smoke of the First World War, every life that moves towards death grows passionately ...... From dusty and dangerous coal mines to glittering royal palaces, from corridors that represent power to bedrooms where love and hate are entwined, the very different and entangled destinies of five families are gradually revealed, in a dramatic display of the 20th century as we think we know it but have never felt it before. The story is a sweeping portrait of a 20th century that we thought we knew, but never felt so truly.
